Biriyani - The Dish That Connects India

Description

Travel accross the different states of India and you'll experience vastly different cultures, habits and languages. Peoples beliefs, habits at times swing to almost opposite from state to state. Yet, there's one thing that you'll find in almost all the states - the Biriyani.

Born somewhere in the kitchens of the Mughal rulers, this aromatic rice dish is loved by all people in all the Indian states. Its true that the dish itself varies subtly in aroma, taste and texture from state to state. Yet the basic flavours and ingradients remains the same.

Ingradients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mutton
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green chillies
  • Ghee
  • Nutmeg
  • Jawitri
  • Bay leaves
  • Salt
  • Saffron
  • Sweet attar

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil the rice and cook in 75%.
  2. Make the mutton
  3. Mix the rice and mutton
  4. Put on the flame and cook till done
